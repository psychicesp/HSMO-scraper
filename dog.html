Adoptable Pets

COVID-19 UPDATES: Adoptions and animal intake are available by appointment only at all HSMO locations. Please visit hsmo.org/covid19 for the latest information and additional updates.

Meet your at HSMO new best friend

Interested in adopting one of our available pets?

Please submit an application for either our Macklind Ave Headquarters or Best Buddy Pet Center locations. Please have the A Number (A followed by 6 letters) for the pet youre interested in adopting. Once we receive your application, you will be added to a list of potential adopters for that pet.

Adoptions are available by appointment only at all HSMO locations. Thank you for your interest in adoption!
